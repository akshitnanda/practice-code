# AlphaForge Product Brief

## ICP

- Crypto-native traders
- Swing traders in US equities
- 2-10 person research pods / boutique funds

## Jobs-to-be-done

1. "Tell me what changed in the last 6 hours that matters for my watchlist."
2. "Show me only high-conviction setups where technicals + catalyst + flow align."
3. "Generate a structured trade plan and stress test scenarios."

## Monetization

- **Free**: delayed data + limited daily AI queries.
- **Pro ($99/mo)**: real-time alerts, advanced screeners, strategy journals.
- **Team ($499/mo+)**: shared workspaces, API access, custom connectors.

## Defensibility

- Proprietary labeled dataset of catalyst-to-price impact.
- Feedback-tuned ranking model for "useful" alerts.
- Explainability format that combines sources + uncertainty.
